# Case000 Image Prompt: Mamiya Reiji

Create a cinematic near-future Japanese cyberpunk audit-record portrait for Persona Null / KASUMI-GATE-09.

Subject:
Mamiya Reiji, a male urban security bureau operator in his late 30s to early 40s. Japanese man. Short black hair with a few gray strands. Tired, restrained expression. He faces the camera like an official forensic database image. He should not look like a villain or a hero. His guilt must remain ambiguous.

Clothing and body:
Dark practical security-bureau uniform jacket. No flashy armor, no fantasy military design.
His right arm is a security-grade partial prosthetic arm, visible from shoulder to wrist. It has matte black synthetic panels, subtle diagnostic seams, neural connector details, and very small pale-cyan status lights.
He is not a full robot. Only the right arm, visual assist, and reaction-support systems are cybernetic.
Add a minimal visual-assist device near one eye, understated and practical.

World and mood:
The image must feel like evidence preserved inside an official audit terminal.
Cold administrative atmosphere. Dark gray, black, white, and pale cyan only.
Subtle scanlines, faint forensic data grid, rain reflected on glass behind him.
No large readable text. No logos. No existing movie, anime, or game references.

Theme:
Ambiguous responsibility.
The viewer should feel: the body fired, the signature remained, but the operator may not have been the true subject controlling the act.

Composition:
16:9 horizontal image.
Centered upper-body portrait.
Enough negative space around the character for UI cropping and profile-card placement.
High detail, realistic but slightly stylized indie game key art.
Cinematic low-key lighting, low saturation, cold color grading.

Negative:
bright neon, cyberpunk nightclub, heroic pose, action scene, anime exaggeration, cute style, smiling, gore, blood, corpse, full robot, giant mecha, fantasy armor, police logo, real-world logo, readable text blocks, distorted hands, extra fingers, overdesigned armor.
